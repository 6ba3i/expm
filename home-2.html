Get Inspired

Helping clients truly experience Italy in a unique and memorable way.

We plan bespoke itineraries with a focus on authentic, captivating and unforgettable experiences. While there are several ways to see Italy, we give our travelers an insider's view of the country. Whether you are an art-lover, a food & wine enthusiast or a history buff, we will design a trip that immerses you and your senses in a journey you will never forget, creating memories that last a lifetime.

What we do

Like a tailor crafting suits, we design customized itineraries. Every trip is tailored to our travelers and their individual needs and interests. We leverage our local expertise and our extensive network to unveil the authentic side of Italy. Hence our name, Authenticus Italy, which traces back to Latin roots: Authenticus is the Latin word for authentic, which is the side of the country we want to show our travelers.

From personalized selection of hotels, transportation and experiences, to continuous support throughout your trip, we are with you from the moment you start dreaming about your trip to the time you leave Italy with everlasting memories.

About us

Authenticus Italy was born from the desire to show the beauty of our country and the uniqueness of our culture to travelers from around the world. From must-see sites to hidden gems, our philosophy is to showcase the authentic Italian essence through personalized and unique experiences.
